Delete Your Prescription Saver Account

Prescription Saver allows you to permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Prescription Saver app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your prescriptions will be deleted.
  • Your reminders will be deleted.
  • Your profile information will be deleted.
  • You will no longer be able to access your account.

Data Retention

After your account deletion request is completed, your personal data is permanently deleted from our systems unless we are required by law to retain certain information.
